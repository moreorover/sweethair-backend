    Looking to up your wrist wear game? Check out our fantastic selection of men's watches. We've got quality models in a variety of styles and designs to suit everyone. Whether you're after something smart and sophisticated you can wear to work, something durable and practical to accompany you whilst you're always on the go or maybe you want something that can keep up with you whilst you're at the gym or swimming pool.

    Timeless watches  

    We've got a variety of luxury designer brands in our watch collection - including stylish models to suit all budgets. Why not take a look at our Casio watches? They're built for timeless and can be worn with any outfit, so whether your dressing up and going formal or keeping it casual, they're a versatile option.

    Water resistant watches

    For those who lead active lifestyles, whether it be extreme water sports that require 200m plus of resistance, swim-proof watches for the occasional dip in the pool, to splash proof watches for sweaty gym fanatics. Browse our fantastic range to find different levels of water resistance to match your lifestyle and needs.  

    Choosing the perfect strap material 

    Alternatively, check out our Accurist men's watches for classic style that looks great when paired with a smart outfit; go for a stainless steel bracelet-style watch will be the perfect complement to your work suits or formal-wear. Our men's Hugo Boss watches are another great choice when it comes to designer style; even if you opt for a more casual fabric strap, you can't fail to look sharp when you're sporting one of these on your wrist. There are some unspoken rules according to watch designers and experts; when it comes to watch straps the basic rules are: black leather straps are for formal wear, metal straps are ideal for the office, and a chronograph strap should be reserved for the weekend. But who says you can't tick to the beat of your own watch and wear your favourite piece wherever and whenever you please., we say if you love it, flaunt it! 

    Smart watches for men 

    Want something that does it all? Consider one of our smart watches. A smart watch allows you to do so much more than just tell the time. You can keep track of your health by monitoring your heart rate, workouts and some high tech smart watches allow you to stay connected by receiving text messages and social media notifications. An Apple watch is a brilliant choice, even featuring a built-in GPS for route mapping capabilities.  Shopping for the whole family? 

    Have a look at our ladies' watches for styles that will enhance your style whether you are looking for a sophisticated bangle watch or a stylish leather strap style. Kids' watches are colourful, help them learn how to tell the time and help keep everyone stylishly punctual.
